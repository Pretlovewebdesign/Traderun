TradeRun FAQs

Are there any delivery restrictions?

  • Currently TradeRun is limited to payloads which can be transported via standard vehicles i.e. sedans or SUVs.
  • TradeRun will look to expand its operations to incorporate light utility utes and trucks into their delivery program to bring you a larger selection of products.

Are there minimum orders?

  • Nope! There are no minimum orders.

How does pricing work?

  • TradeRun uses GPS data to provide customers an estimated fee inclusive of the delivery and service cost. Once the customer finalises their ‘Buy List’ the location coordinates of the customer and supplier will be calculated which the customer will confirm prior to payment.
  • Once the order has been delivered, an updated invoice will be supplied to the customer to reflect their purchase and delivery cost.

What about delivery times?

  • TradeRun is on-demand and can generally have items delivered within 40 minutes of supplier approval.*
  • We are available to register orders 24/7, however, we are subject to the supplier’s hours of operation.

(*) Delivery timeframe can differ depending on item availability and customer location.

I need something not listed in the TradeRun app! Can I make custom orders?

  • Although TradeRun’s first service offering does not include custom orders, customers can recommend products to be included under the ‘request to add item’ button in ‘settings’.

What is the payment process?

  • We accept all major credit cards. All payments have security protection for quick payments and peace of mind. You can store several credit cards with TradeRun, who will prompt you to confirm the correct card for each transaction.

Who picks up my order?

  • We have trusted partnerships with accredited companies who run extensive background checks on their drivers to monitor registration compliance and adequate training.

What if there are any problems with my order?

  • TradeRun offers an in-app chat function which customers can use to communicate problems or issues. A customer service representative is available 8am – 5pm, Monday – Friday.

What happens if I need to return an item?

  • Returns are accepted by the issuing supplier using the invoice number of the transaction.
  • It is the responsibility of the customer to return any product(s) or item(s) to the correct supplier.
  • All refunds will be returned to the customers nominated bank account or credit card provided.
  • Refund payments cannot be issued in-store and must be completed through TradeRun in order to satisfy accounting and book-keeping purposes.
