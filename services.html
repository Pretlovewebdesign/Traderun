Service Description

TradeRun’s mobile application service is developed for day to day use by tradespeople within the building and construction industry. TradeRun utilises a stylish but simplistic mobile application, available on both Android and iOS, to source and deliver supplies and materials directly to the end customer. Customers can simply browse product inventories either through industry classifications, trade categories or by individual products and begin to compile their ‘Buy List’.

Once the ‘Buy List’ has been complied, TradeRun’s sophisticated software will source the required items from reputable wholesale suppliers and provide customers with an invoice, delivery cost estimations and an expected delivery timeframe. TradeRun integrates leading online banking systems to offer fast and secure payments for each of their customers purchases, backed with an insurance guarantee of $10,000 on each order. Customers are able to monitor their orders via the TradeRun app which updates expected delivery times to ensure customer deadlines are met.

The in-app Account Manager allows users to view account summaries, allocate invoices and job numbers, view current and completed orders, and add or change account information including; personal information, credit card details or company information. TradeRun removes the stress and labour pressures involved with traditional material and supply runs, no longer needing to leave job sites, customers can now access entire wholesale inventories at the touch of a button and have their products delivered directly to the door.

Wholesale suppliers operate on a scheduled delivery service, generally next day delivery depending on staff availability and the customer delivery backlog. TradeRun’s unique on-demand service is unmatched by traditional methods, as customers can get immediate delivery. Adding to the company’s value, TradeRun will hold accounts with multiple leading wholesale suppliers resulting in customers needing only one TradeRun account. This method will ensure customers have the advantage of dealing with one company and gain the buying power of a large organisation. When looking at productivity downtime and the opportunity cost of the customer to complete their own supply runs, it becomes clear that TradeRun will have an immediate and long-lasting effect on the building and construction industry. Wholesale suppliers also gain value, as a reduction in customer walk-ins and in-house deliveries will result in a streamlined and cost-effective business model.
